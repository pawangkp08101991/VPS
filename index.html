About Our School

Vikramaditya Public School is an English-medium, private unaided institution located in Bhathat, Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh. Founded in 2019, VPS is committed to providing a safe and clean campus alongside a strong academic environment. We believe every child deserves quality education at a reasonable cost, and our experienced teaching staff works closely with each student to build confidence, curiosity, and character.
