About Our School

Vikramaditya Public School, Gorakhpur (UP) is committed to providing quality education in a nurturing and disciplined environment. With experienced faculty, modern infrastructure and a curriculum that balances academics with sports, arts and values, we prepare every student to become a confident, responsible citizen ready to take on tomorrow's challenges.
