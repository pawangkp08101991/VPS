About Our School

Vikramaditya Public School, Gorakhpur (UP) has been nurturing young minds since its establishment, blending strong academic foundations with values, discipline and creativity. We follow a CBSE-aligned curriculum delivered by experienced, caring teachers in a safe and joyful campus, supported by well-equipped science and computer labs, a library, sports facilities and smart classrooms. Our mission is to build confident, responsible and future-ready students who excel not only in academics but also in sports, arts and life skills.
